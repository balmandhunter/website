Travel

wandering the earth

I have always loved exploring new places. I was in diapers on my first international trip, and I have been enamored with traveling for as long as I can remember. The amount of natural and man-made beauty in the world is staggering. As much as I enjoy visiting unique places, developing friendships with the people I meet along the way is even more precious. Every time I experience a new culture, I see the world from a slightly different perspective. There is no better way to learn than through experience, and my thirst for knowledge will never be quenched.
